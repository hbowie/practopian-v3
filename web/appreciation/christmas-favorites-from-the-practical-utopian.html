Christmas Favorites from The Practical Utopian

20 Dec 2020 · 1 min read

topics: Christmashumanism

The Christmas season is one of my favorite times of year, and I’ve acquired a huge collection of holiday music over the years, so I thought I would share my thoughts about a few of my favorites with all of you. So here are 20 of my favorite holiday tracks. I’ve tried to select as wide a variety of songs as possible, mixing different artists and styles.

  • The Rebel Jesus - Song by Jackson Browne
  • Father Christmas - Song by The Kinks
  • Merry Christmas Baby - Composed by Charles Brown, performed by Otis Redding
  • Christmas Time Back Home - Song by The Country Gentlemen
  • Please Come Home for Christmas
  • Christmas (Baby Please Come Home) - Song by Darlene Love and Phil Spector
  • Holiday in Harlem
  • Christmas Night in Harlem
  • Christmas Bells by John Gorka
  • Christmas Must Be Tonight by The Band
  • Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas
  • Christmas Song by Dave Mattthews
  • Auld Lang Syne
  • The Little Drummer Boy
  • My Favorite Things
  • Star of Wonder by The Roches
  • Christmas Time’s A-Coming
  • Boogie Woogie Santa Claus by Mabel Scott
  • The Christmas Song
  • Santa Claus is Coming to Town

And all of these songs but one can be heard on Apple Music in the Christmas Favorites from The Practical Utopian playlist.

Enjoy!
